## Category: careers
## Subcategory: all

## Join Our Team
We're always looking for talented individuals passionate about technology and innovation. At Minterminds, you'll work on challenging projects, collaborate with a skilled team, and grow your career.

## Current Opportunities
We regularly hire for positions in:
- **Software Development**: Frontend, Backend, Full Stack, Mobile
- **UI/UX Design**: User Research, UI Design, UX Design
- **Project Management**: Agile Project Management, Scrum Master
- **Quality Assurance**: Manual and Automated Testing
- **DevOps**: Cloud Infrastructure, CI/CD, Automation
- **Digital Marketing**: SEO, Social Media, Content Marketing
- **Blockchain Development**: Smart Contracts, DApps
- **Business Development**: Client Relations, Sales

## What We Offer
- **Meaningful Work**: Projects that make a real impact
- **Learning Culture**: Continuous learning and skill development
- **Flexible Environment**: Remote and hybrid work options
- **Collaborative Team**: Supportive and talented colleagues
- **Growth Opportunities**: Clear career progression paths
- **Competitive Compensation**: Market-competitive salaries and benefits

## Hiring Policies
- ✅ We hire fresh graduates with strong fundamentals
- ✅ We offer remote and hybrid work arrangements
- ✅ We provide comprehensive onboarding and training
- ✅ We encourage career growth and progression
- ✅ We value teamwork and knowledge sharing
- ✅ We offer performance-based career advancement

## Application Process
1. **Submit Application**: Resume, cover letter, portfolio/GitHub
2. **Initial Screening**: Brief call to discuss background
3. **Technical Assessment**: Practical evaluation of skills
4. **Team Interviews**: Meetings with team members and leadership
5. **Final Offer**: Competitive package and onboarding

## Team Culture
- Regular team events and knowledge sharing sessions
- Opportunities to work on diverse projects
- Support for certifications and further education
- Flat hierarchy and open communication
- Work-life balance and flexible schedules
- Internal tech workshops and learning sessions

## FAQ
**Q: How can I apply for a position?**
A: Send your resume and portfolio to careers@minterminds.com or apply through our careers portal.

**Q: What should I include in my application?**
A: Resume, cover letter, portfolio or GitHub profile showcasing your work.

**Q: Do you offer remote work?**
A: Yes, we offer flexible remote and hybrid work arrangements.

**Q: What's the typical interview process?**
A: Initial screening, technical assessment, team interviews, and final discussion with leadership.

**Q: Do you hire fresh graduates?**
A: Yes, we welcome applications from talented fresh graduates with strong fundamentals and passion.

**Q: How long does the hiring process take?**
A: Typically 2-3 weeks from initial application to offer.

**Q: Do you conduct interviews online?**
A: Yes, initial rounds may be conducted virtually.

**Q: Is there training during onboarding?**
A: Yes, trainees receive guidance and skill development.

**Q: Are part-time roles available?**
A: Occasionally, based on project needs.

**Q: Will I get a recommendation letter after internship?**
A: High performers may receive recommendation letters.

**Q: Can I apply directly on the website?**
A: Yes, you can contact HR via the contact form or email.

**Q: Are backend development roles available?**
A: Yes, we regularly hire backend engineers.

**Q: Do you hire UI/UX designers?**
A: Yes, UI and graphic designers are welcome.

**Q: Are mobile app developer positions open?**
A: Yes, mobile app developers are part of our regular hiring.

**Q: Do you offer internships?**
A: Yes, internships in software, app development, and design are offered.

**Q: How long are internships?**
A: Internship duration varies by program and project, typically 3-6 months.

**Q: Do interns get mentorship?**
A: Yes, interns receive mentorship from senior developers.

**Q: Can interns work on real projects?**
A: Yes, interns contribute to live projects under supervision.

**Q: Are internships paid?**
A: Some internships may offer stipends depending on role and performance.

**Q: Can interns get full-time offers?**
A: Yes, strong interns may be considered for full-time roles.
